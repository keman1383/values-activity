What Matters Most to You?

You'll be shown a series of personal values one at a time. For each one, decide: Is this important to me? Keep the ones that resonate. Then you'll narrow your list down to your top 10 — and finally your top 5.

how it works

There are no right or wrong answers. Trust your instincts and choose what genuinely reflects your priorities, not what you think you should value.
